Description

The chain alignments between this assembly and other published assemblies.

Methods

Assemblies were aligned with minimap2. For intra-species alignments, minimap2 was run with -x asm5 --secondary=no. For inter-species alignments, minimap2 was run with -x asm20 --eqx -c --cs -k 15 -m 10 -A 1 -B 2 -O2,12 -n2 -g 100 -r 200,100000 --secondary=no -s 1000. The results were converted into chain format using transanno minimap2chain.
